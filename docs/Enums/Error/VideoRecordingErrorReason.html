VideoRecordingErrorReason

public enum VideoRecordingErrorReason

Represents the various reasons a video recording error would be reported.

  • unsupported

    Garage doesn’t support video recording. Testing video recording on simulator seems to not be supported by the Framework and the asset writer status to remain equal to unknown even if it has attempted to start recording

  • noRecordingInProgress

    Garage tried to stop recording video while there was no video recording in progress
